Our mission

To encourage the creation and distribution of eBooks.

— Michael Hart, founder, 1971

Free knowledge, for everyone

Michael Hart invented eBooks in 1971 and his memory continues to inspire the creation of eBooks and related content today. Since then, thousands of volunteers have digitized and diligently proofread the world's literature.

All Project Gutenberg eBooks are completely free and always will be. A 2004 essay by Michael Hart provides more detail on the mission statement and the beliefs that guide Project Gutenberg's activities.
